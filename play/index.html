Mining Session

Set mining session SOL

Mining can create or expand Solana chunk storage accounts. This SOL is sent from your wallet to your browser session signer so mining can submit transactions without asking you to sign every block.

  • The spending is Solana network cost: transaction fees and rent-exempt storage deposits for chunk PDAs.
  • It is not paid to the NiceChunk project wallet, and the project cannot withdraw your session balance.
  • Your main wallet only signs this setup or a later top-up; block mining is then signed by your temporary session key.
