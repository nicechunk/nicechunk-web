NiceChunk Roadbook

Roadbook for a player-owned voxel world.

NiceChunk is moving from long-form design into launch preparation: a seeded voxel world, public rules, browser gameplay, resource foundations, wallet identity, and a future Guardian network built around verifiable game state.

This roadbook is written for players, builders, guilds, creators, and ecosystem partners who want a clear view of what is live, what is being prepared, and which risks are being handled openly.

      Trust Architecture

      Confidence comes from visible rules, not slogans.

      NiceChunk should be judged by what players can inspect: world generation, resource foundations, wallet boundaries, action rules, chain storage direction, and the limits of what is live today.

      Planning Confidence

      Near-term work is more certain. Long-term systems stay adaptive.

      Security & Compliance

      Game-first, transparent, and careful about promises.

      Web3 games earn trust by shipping playable systems, publishing rules, protecting players, and avoiding financial promises. NiceChunk treats digital assets as game-state and identity extensions, not guaranteed-return products.

        Digital assets may fluctuate in value. NiceChunk does not promise profit, buybacks, fixed rewards, or asset appreciation. Users should evaluate participation risk according to their own jurisdiction.
